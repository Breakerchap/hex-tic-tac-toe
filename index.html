Infinite Hex Tic-Tac-Toe

Connect 6 in a straight line on one of the three hex axes. The first move must be at the origin, then every placement must stay within 11 hexes of an existing tile. Player 1 opens with 1 move, then every turn uses 2.

Rules & controls
  • Click a hex to place.
  • The first move must be at (0, 0).
  • Later placements must be within 11 hexes of an existing tile.
  • Drag with right mouse button or middle mouse to pan.
  • Scroll to zoom.
  • Winning line length is always 6.
  • The opening turn is always 1 placement, no matter who starts.
